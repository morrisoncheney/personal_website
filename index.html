✦ Chronicle ✦

Forged somewhere between the deserts of the Middle East and the mountains of Utah, Morrison Cheney is a Machine Learning artificer and Mathematics scholar currently studying at Brigham Young University. Having spent six years living in Kuwait and Jordan — and wandered through more than 30 kingdoms across the known world — he brings a rare kind of perspective to every problem he encounters.

By day, he works in the research halls of DRAGN Labs, helping to shape a novel ~70 billion-parameter language model and building PyTorch training pipelines for AI systems modeled after biological neural mechanisms. By night, he's likely wrestling with mathematics, picking up a guitar, or plotting the next great expedition.

Conversationally fluent in Persian (Farsi). Seasoned leader. Veteran of 2 years of volunteer service across Bulgaria and Germany.
